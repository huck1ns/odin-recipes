Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti dish is a simple and comforting meal that can be made in under an hour. It contains sausage and a red sauce, perfect for dinner and meal prep.

Ingredients

  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Italian Sausage
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Spaghetti Pasta

Steps

  1. Cut sausage and add to pot on medium heat until outside browns.
  2. Dice onions and peppers and saute with sausage.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and stir.
  4. Add carrot, finely diced or cut into thin strips.
  5. Add garlic and seasoning, stir.
  6. Let cook until taste is satifying.
  7. Take cooked spaghetti, add sauce.
